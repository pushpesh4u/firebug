This test-case is intended for Issue #2297 - Reporting failure to load javascript.
Jan Odvarko, odvarko@gmail.com

  1. Open Firebug and enable the Net panel.
  2. Switch to the Console panel and use the options menu to enable Show Network Errors option.
  3. Reload this page.
  4. You should see a NetworkError in the Console panel and the status bar should display 1 Error.
  5. Click on the error and you shoud be navigated into the Net panel where the appropriate entry (displayed in red color) should be opened.
